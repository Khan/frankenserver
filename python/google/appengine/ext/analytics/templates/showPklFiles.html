Appstats Analytics

This tool is intended to help you analyze the performance of your application. The tool analyzes data recorded by the Appstats tool, and helps you visualize a variety of metrics such as the distribution of response times of requests for different URLs, global views of RPCs invoked by each request and times taken by them, entity access counts, and entity group access counts.
